WIFI SETUP

Before doing fun things like voice
recognition you will need to setup 
your WiFi.

Select Edit->WiFi from main menu bar.

In the form that appears:
-Enter the SSID for your WiFi station.
-Enter the passcode for your WiFi station.
-Press OK to save WiFi settings. 

The WiFi connection should connect in a
few seconds. If it doesn't, try rebooting.
